Seed Garden - spring garden beds!





Spring is upon us! At Seed Garden, we've put together this little document to show you around some of our beds and help you out a bit with your own gardening. Some beds have had their photo taken, so you can virtually explore. You can also take a look around the greenhouse.

Click on the plants in the "Beds" tab. You'll notice certain plants are circled. Just click on those plants to find out more about what they are, how to plant them, and how to care for them.
